Welcome to your first lab!

You have a very small laboratory to start producing your stem cell therapies! Get the place up and running by hiring specialized workers and buying incubators and safety cabinets in order to process stem cell donors. From each donor you will get one million cells. You have been requested to cure 10 people and for each cure you will need 75 million cells. The set price for each therapy is €20000. Make sure you manage to get a reputation score above 50 and a net profit from sales of at least €10000.
